Can I get a free trial before I purchase?
ans: Yes. Free trial automatically converts into a 1-month membership for $3.99/month, unless you turn off automatic renewal before the end of the free trial.

How do I create a Return Request?
ans: You can create a Return in three simple steps

1. Tap on MyOrders
2. Choose the item to be Returned
3. Enter details requested and create a return request

How do I check the status of my order?
ans: Please tap on “My Orders” section under main menu of App/Website/M-site to check your order status.
